Laravel Deploy Actions

open in new windowopen in new windowopen in new windowopen in new window

Actions are like version control for your actions process, allowing your team to modify and share the application's actionable schema. If you have ever had to tell a teammate to manually perform any action on a production server, you've come across an issue that actions solves.
